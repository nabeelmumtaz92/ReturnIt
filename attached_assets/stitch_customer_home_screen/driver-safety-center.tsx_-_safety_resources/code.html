Safety Resources

Safe Driving Practices

expand_more
  • Always wear your seatbelt.
  • Maintain a safe following distance.
  • Avoid distractions like phone use.
  • Obey all traffic laws and speed limits.
  • Be aware of your surroundings and potential hazards.

Emergency Procedures

expand_more
  • Ensure your safety and the safety of others first.
  • Call emergency services immediately.
  • Exchange information with other parties involved.
  • Contact Return It support to report the incident.

Package Handling

expand_more
  • Secure packages properly to prevent shifting.
  • Use appropriate lifting techniques to avoid injury.
  • Be cautious when delivering to ensure safety.
  • Follow Return It's guidelines for damaged packages.

Roadside & Support

For immediate assistance, contact Return It support. Our team is available 24/7.
