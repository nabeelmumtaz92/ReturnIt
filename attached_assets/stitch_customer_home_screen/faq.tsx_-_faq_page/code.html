Return It Help Center

Frequently Asked Questions

General

Return It is the category leader in reverse logistics, delivery, and returns. Our platform powers the return economy with a sophisticated, easy-to-use system for everyone involved.

Customer

To initiate a return, log into your account, go to the 'Orders' section, select the item you wish to return, and follow the on-screen instructions to generate a shipping label.

Driver

Driver earnings are calculated based on distance, package size, and time. Payouts are processed weekly directly to your linked bank account.

Payments

We accept all major credit cards, PayPal, and Apple Pay. All transactions are securely processed through our encrypted payment gateway.
