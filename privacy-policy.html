ReturnIt Privacy Policy

Effective Date: September 5, 2025

1. Information We Collect

When you use ReturnIt, we collect:

  • Account Information: Name, email address, phone number
  • Location Data: GPS coordinates for pickup and delivery tracking
  • Package Information: Photos and details of items being returned
  • Payment Information: Processed securely through Stripe
  • Usage Data: App usage patterns and preferences

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide pickup and delivery services
  • Track packages and provide real-time updates
  • Process payments securely
  • Communicate about your orders
  • Improve our services
  • Ensure driver and customer safety

3. Information Sharing

We share your information only when necessary:

  • With Drivers: Contact details and pickup/delivery locations
  • Payment Processing: Stripe for secure payment handling
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law
  • Service Providers: Third-party services that help operate our platform

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including:

  • Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS)
  • Secure cloud storage
  • Access controls and authentication
  • Regular security audits

5. Location Data

ReturnIt uses location services to:

  • Enable pickup and delivery tracking
  • Provide real-time location updates
  • Optimize driver routes
  • Ensure accurate service delivery

You can disable location services in your device settings, but this may limit app functionality.

6. Data Retention

We retain your data only as long as necessary to provide services and comply with legal obligations. Order history is kept for 2 years for tax and business purposes.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Update or correct your information
  • Delete your account and data
  • Opt out of marketing communications
  • Request data portability

8. Children's Privacy

ReturnIt is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes via email or through the app.
